Where this is going

When I started, I thought design was mostly about visual craft. Over the years I learned that most design problems are decision problems: people trying to agree on what to build and why.

AI changed the part in between. Turning a decision into working software got fast — I've spent this year shipping my own design changes to production. What didn't get faster is the ends: understanding what's worth building, and checking whether it worked for a real person. That's where I put my time.
