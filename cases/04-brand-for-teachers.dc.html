The concept What it includes Why it's here
TL;DR — Planificando had no visual identity beyond a logo and a default blue. I designed the brand from scratch in December 2025: concept, palette, shapes, and a usage manual. It was then applied to the landing page, the product interface, tutorials, and printed materials for school workshops.

The concept

Two ideas anchor the identity. Yellow alongside the blue, with a shape suggesting a sun: a product made in and for Argentina. And two families of shapes: geometric ones alluding to AI, organic ones alluding to the classroom.

Palette / shape samples from the manual

What it includes

Brand manual, color palette, typography, shape system, and application rules. Applied to: landing page (redesigned January 2026), product UI (unified design system, May 2026), video tutorials, LinkedIn graphics, and workshop merchandising.
